# Process Customer Research

Use the Task tool to invoke the product-manager agent to synthesize customer research into actionable insights.

`subagent_type: product-manager`

**Prompt**: "Synthesize customer research from $ARGUMENTS into patterns, key insights (with quotes and frequency), and recommended actions. Write to `/docs/product/research/[date]-[topic]-insights.md`."
