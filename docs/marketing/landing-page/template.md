## Design Handoff Notes

```markdown
## Design Handoff Notes for product-design-lead

**Visual Hierarchy** (you own final layout decisions):
1. Headline (largest, boldest)
2. Primary CTA (high contrast color, prominent)
3. Trust signals (visible but not overwhelming)
4. Body copy (readable, scannable)

**CTA Strategy**:
- **Placement**: Hero, after each section (6 total)
- **Color**: High contrast with background
- **Size**: 44x44px minimum tap target (mobile)
- **Text**: First-person, action-oriented

**Mobile-First Requirements**:
- Font size: 16px minimum (body), 28px+ (headlines)
- Line height: 1.5+ for readability
- Narrow columns: 45-90 characters per line
- Left-aligned text only
- No carousels/sliders (YC principle: kills conversions)

**Layout Suggestions** (you own final decisions):
- Hero: Full viewport height, video/demo prominent
- Sections: Generous whitespace, clear separation
- Social proof: Card layout, photos prominent
- Pricing: Table or card grid, highlight popular tier
- FAQ: Accordion or simple list

**Color Psychology**:
- CTA: Action colors (green for go, blue for trust)
- Trust signals: Blue (trust), gold (premium)
- Background: High contrast with text (WCAG AA minimum)
```
